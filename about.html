About Vietcong

Vietcong is a first-person shooter game developed by Pterodon and Illusion Softworks, released in 2003. Set during the Vietnam War, players take on the role of Sergeant First Class Steve R. Hawkins, leading a U.S. Special Forces team through various jungle missions. The game emphasizes realism with authentic weapons, equipment, and environments, and focuses on team-based tactics, allowing players to command their squad members effectively. The immersive storyline and atmospheric setting provide a gripping depiction of the war's challenges and intensity.
