Addons

Vietcong allows adding addons to the game

How to add addon?

  1. Download addon
  2. Extract addon to addons folder (If doesn't exists, create it)
  3. Open VcComHub and hit "quick play" button, and choose addon

Here is some list of addons:

FistAplha [GD] RedDawn [GD] ZuluAddon [GD]
